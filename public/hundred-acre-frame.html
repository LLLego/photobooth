A few decisions worth flagging: - **Center is a real window, not just blank space.** A semi-translucent cream panel with `inset 0 4px 24px rgba(74,37,21,0.18)` gives the photo area a subtle framed-mat look, so when a real photo is composited in, it won't fight the border. The 📷 is just a placeholder marker. - **The corner vignettes use `conic-gradient` as a honeycomb suggestion.** Six segments rotating gold → honey → brown read as蜂窝 cells at a glance, and the inner cream radial-gradient keeps each character lifted off the busy ring. Each character is set at 120–130px so they clearly dominate the corner, not nestle inside it. - **The title gets a triple-stacked `text-shadow`** (gold → honey → dark-brown blur) instead of a flat drop-shadow — that's the cheap-but-effective trick to make serif italic feel like it was pressed into warm foil. The little "EST. FOREVER" sub-tag and heart row anchor the bottom so it doesn't feel like a floating word. - **Honey pots at top-center are clustered and tilted inward** (`-22deg` / `+22deg`) rather than three-in-a-row — a small composition tell that separates "designed" from "dropped in".
