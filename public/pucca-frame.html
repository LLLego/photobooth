A photobooth frame felt like the perfect place to lean into the cute cartoon energy — so I kept the dark moody intelligence for the *body* (the booth's exterior aesthetic) and let the inner frame explode with bright red/pink cartoon energy. The two characters are built entirely from CSS shapes: Pucca with her signature black hair buns, blunt bangs, asymmetrical pigtails and red dress; Garu as a ninja with his pointed dark hair, mask, and a subtle hint of his eyes peeking through.
