A piglet photobooth, but make it bloom — that's where my head went. Soft warm light, hand-illustrated pig built entirely from CSS (no PNGs, no SVG sprites — every curl of the tail is a border-radius), and a `#FFB6C1 / #FF69B4` palette held in check by quiet cream tones so the pink reads warm, not synthetic. The camera glyph sits as a placeholder in the empty centre, waiting for a real shot. Type does most of the quiet work: Cormorant Garamond italic for the brand mark and date, Inter for the small UPPERCASE labels with wide tracking, and the big `PIGLET` lockup mixes the two so the word feels handwritten, not stamped. Dotted inner hairline + a soft shadow inside the photo well gives the frame that paper-photo weight. Motion is restrained — the pig bobs gently, its tail unfurls once, the camera keeps a slow breath — nothing pulses, nothing flashes. Piglet · Photobooth
