A few notes on the build: - **Polaroid proportions** follow the real thing: 18px white border on top/sides, ~78px at the bottom for the handwriting zone — that's where the iconic bottom-heavy white lip comes from. - **Double border** is the outer cream edge plus the inset terracotta line at 6px (`::before`) — clean and structural rather than decorative. - **Center stays open** as a soft tonal wash with a dashed outline and a small circle guide icon, so it reads as "drop a photo here" without ever filling the space. - **CSS-only characters** dotting the edges: a radiating sun, two leaves on a stem, two daisies, two butterflies with dashed flight trails, a tiny heart, and small crosses and dots for rhythm. Every piece is built from `div`s with `border-radius`, `transform`, and `clip-path`-style rotation — no SVG, no images.
